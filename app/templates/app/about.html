Traveler, writer, photographer, foodie, exCouchSurfer, BeWelcomer, HelperX, dreamer

I am from a small town in Estonia. I grew up reading travel literature and flipping through hotels and restaurants brochures. While studying in university I started to work in bars, restaurants and hotels, perfect places to meet travelers and hear their amazing and inspiring stories. After graduating I packed my seven things and since then I am official backpacker. My drawers are filled with full-written notebooks and photos have occupied my external hard drives. In this blog I write about my favourite experiences, great food and interesting sights. Everything is written from my perspective so everyone are free to agree or disagree.
